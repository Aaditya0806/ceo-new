Accounting Services

Structured Accounting & Financial Management Services for Growing Businesses

Every business — whether a startup, trading firm, ecommerce brand, manufacturer, or service provider — needs reliable accounting to grow with confidence.

Clean books, timely reports, and financial clarity make it easier to:

Make informed decisions

Attract investors

Manage cashflow effectively

Maintain statutory compliance

Scale operations with confidence

At CEO HR Consultancy, our Professional Accounting Services are designed to give business owners complete peace of mind. We combine structured processes, deep financial domain expertise, and modern accounting systems to deliver accurate, timely, and insight-driven financial reporting.

Why Professional Accounting Services Matter

Accounting is not just about recording transactions. It is about understanding the financial health of your business.

A professionally maintained accounting system helps answer critical business questions:

Are you consistently profitable?

Which costs are increasing month-over-month?

How strong is your cash position?

Are receivables getting delayed?

Is your business financially ready to expand?

Most founders and business owners do not have the time to monitor these financial details daily — and that’s where structured professional accounting support becomes essential.

Our approach ensures that your books are:

✔ Accurate

✔ Updated regularly

✔ Transparent

✔ Audit-ready

✔ Decision-supportive

Our Accounting Services Include

1. Monthly Bookkeeping

Recording all sales, purchases, expenses, and payments

Bank and credit card reconciliation

Vendor and customer ledger maintenance

GST-related accounting entries

Ensuring systematic documentation

We maintain clean, structured, and compliant books that reflect the true financial position of your business

2. Accounts Management

Accounts Payable (Vendor management)

Accounts Receivable (Customer follow-ups & tracking)

Expense tracking & categorization

Cashflow monitoring

Periodic account reviews

We help businesses maintain discipline in financial operations while improving financial visibility.

3. Monthly MIS & Business Performance Reports

Profit & Loss Statement

Balance Sheet

Cost Analysis Reports

Performance comparison (Month-on-Month)

Cashflow Summary

Key financial insights & recommendations

Our MIS reporting is designed not just for compliance — but for better business decision-making.
