Bulk Assessment

Revolutionizing talent evaluation for large-scale recruitment, our Bulk Assessment services combine expertise with cutting-edge technology to deliver precise and reliable results.
With over 5,00,000 assessments conducted and 45,000+ placements, we have the experience to meet high-volume hiring demands. Beyond numbers, we promote diversity and inclusion, helping clients build all-women workforces and enabling opportunities.
Our mobile infrastructure, powered by 500+ smart devices, transforms even remote locations into fully functional digital assessment centers.

Key Features

Psychometric and skill-based assessments

Customized assessment frameworks

Logical reasoning, spatial ability, and technical skills evaluation

Types of Tests

Psycho-Diagnostic Assessments (stress tolerance, focus)

General Aptitude Tests (cognitive abilities)

Domain Proficiency Assessments (technical expertise)

Neuromuscular Tests (physical readiness)

Group Task Exercises (teamwork and leadership)
