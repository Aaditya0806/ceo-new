Key Features of the HPT Org Structure

Flat and Inclusive Design

Eliminates rigid hierarchies, promoting egalitarianism and meaningful contributions.

Functional Leaders (FL) guide core areas: Quality, Cost, Delivery, Availability, Morale, and Safety (QCDSMA).

Team Leaders (TL) and Team Members (TM) collaborate to execute tasks and achieve missions

Key Features of the HPT Org Structure

Centralized Leadership

Business Leaders (BL) provide strategic guidance while delegating decision-making to functional and team leaders.

Value Chain Integration

Teams are organized around value chains, enabling end-to-end operations like planning, material handling, quality assurance, and delivery

Focus on QCDSMA

Integrates Quality, Cost, Delivery, Availability, Morale, and Safety into team goals for holistic performance and growth.

Skill Development

Cross-training programs ensure team members are proficient across multiple functional areas, enhancing adaptability
