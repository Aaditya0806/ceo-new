What is a Shift Assembly Meeting ?

The Shift Assembly Meeting is a structured communication forum conducted at the beginning of every shift. It bridges outgoing and incoming teams, ensuring smooth transitions, enhanced collaboration, and operational ownership. By sharing updates, reviewing performance, and planning tasks, SAM keeps teams informed, engaged, and aligned with organizational objectives.

Purpose of SAM

Communicate Updates

Share key happenings, performance metrics, and business updates.
Provide essential safety and operational information to the incoming team.

Encourage Engagement

Actively involve team members in discussions.
Instill ownership in delivering team targets.

Key Responsibilities in SAM

HR Facilitator / HVAT

Monitors and ensures proper execution of SAM.

Team Members

Attend punctually and participate actively in discussions

Shift Leaders

Conduct the meeting and ensure adherence to the agenda

Meeting Details

Duration

20 minutes

Location

SAM Room

Participants

Outgoing Shift Leader, Incoming Shift Leader, and Team Members

Timings

Start of every shift (6:00 AM, 2:00 PM, 10:00 PM)

Benefits of SAM

Improved Team Alignment

Ensures all members are informed and aligned with goals.

Enhanced Communication

Facilitates effective information sharing and coordination.

Increased Accountability

Fosters a culture of responsibility and transparency.

Streamlined Operations

Reduces inefficiencies through structured discussions and planning.

Agenda of SAM

Safety Updates

Share safety incidents, near misses, and accidents. Highlight new methods or processes for workplace safety.

Safety Manual Reading

Read and explain a page from the safety manual to reinforce safe practices.

Major Happenings in Previous Shifts

Discuss breakdowns, power failures, absenteeism, and significant events.

Technical Communication Book

Review and share relevant technical updates

Customer Complaints

Discuss feedback and corrective action plans.

Shift Matrix

Outline the tasks, resource allocation, and targets for the upcoming shift.

Other Updates

Share motivational thoughts or news headlines to inspire the team

SAM Log Sheet Ensuring Accountability

The log sheet promotes transparency, supports continuous improvement, and provides a reliable record of operational insights.

Safety and Maintenance Updates

Key incidents, near misses, and learnings.

Quality Metrics and Issues

Insights into quality parameters and deviations

Planning and Delivery

Operational plans, task allocations, and progress

Learning of the Day

Key takeaways or new ideas from the meeting

Lean and Engagement Activities

Improvement initiatives and engagement metrics.

Basic Premises of SAM

Standing meeting lasting 15-20 minutes

Punctuality and agenda adherence are critical

Participants come prepared with data for discussion

Major updates are recorded in the SAM log sheet

Communication should be crisp and clear.

Issues are discussed for future resolution but not grievances

Conducted by the Team Captain or Planning & Delivery STAR Cap in their absence
